Tips
\n

\n\tTransient prefix origins are cases of ASes that announce a prefix for a very short time and then withdraw it right afterwards. Cyclops considers such announcements transient if they last for less than 5 days. The start and end dates specify the time window where Cyclops should look for appearances or disappearances of prefixes.\n\t
